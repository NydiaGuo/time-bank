Kids are spending so much time on wasteful activities, and not enough on meaningful things that build character, personable skills, etc. The app tracks the that kids do in order to earn bank time that kids can . The parents set the weight for each activity.

Wouldn't it be great if a kid spent more time doing the meaningful and productive things in life. This is your deposit time.

Does your kid want to watch TV, play video games, or watch videos on his phone. This is the earned time that lets him do those things.
